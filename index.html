A solution made for YOU.

Camp is busy, and so are you. We make running camp a breeze with our suite of tools which are intuitive and made unique for your camp.

Intent is a web-application that can be accessed through any internet browser. It provides the ability to perform every camp task that can be done on a computer, such as camper registration, payment, point-of-sale, health portals, food service scheduling and planning, camper management and organization, staff applications, guest group management, administrative access, reporting, donar portals, and so much more!
